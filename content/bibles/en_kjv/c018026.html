26

1 But Job answered and said 2 How hast thou helped without power savest thou the arm no strength 3 How hast thou counselled no wisdom hast thou plentifully declared the thing as it is 4 To whom hast thou uttered words and whose spirit came 5 Dead are formed from under the waters and the inhabitants 6 Hell naked before him, and destruction hath no covering 7 He stretcheth out the north over the empty place hangeth the earth upon nothing 8 He bindeth up the waters in his thick clouds and the cloud is not rent 9 He holdeth back the face of his throne spreadeth his cloud 10 He hath compassed the waters with bounds until the day and night come to an end 11 The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at his reproof 12 He divideth the sea with his power and by his understanding he smiteth through the proud 13 By his spirit he hath garnished the heavens his hand hath formed the crooked serpent 14 parts of his ways but how little a portion is heard of him? but the thunder of his power who can understand
