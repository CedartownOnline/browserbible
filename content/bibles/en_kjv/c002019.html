1 In the third month when the children of Israel were gone forth out of the land of Egypt the same day came the wilderness of Sinai 2 For they were departed from Rephidim and were come the desert of Sinai and had pitched in the wilderness and there Israel camped before the mount 3 And Moses went up unto God called unto him out of the mountain saying Thus shalt thou say to the house of Jacob and tell the children of Israel 4 Ye have seen what I did unto the Egyptians I bare you on eagles wings and brought 5 Now therefore, if ye will obey my voice indeed and keep my covenant then ye shall be a peculiar treasure unto me above all people for all the earth 6 And ye shall be unto me a kingdom of priests and an holy nation the words which thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel 7 And Moses came and called for the elders of the people and laid before their faces all these words commanded 8 And all the people answered together and said hath spoken we will do And Moses returned the words of the people 9 said unto Moses Lo, I come unto thee in a thick cloud that the people may hear when I speak with thee, and believe thee for ever And Moses told the words of the people 10 said unto Moses Go unto the people and sanctify them to day and to morrow and let them wash their clothes 11 And be ready against the third day for the third day will come down in the sight of all the people upon mount Sinai 12 And thou shalt set bounds unto the people round about saying Take heed up into the mount or touch the border of it: whosoever toucheth the mount shall be surely put to death 13 There shall not an hand touch it, but he shall surely be stoned or shot through beast or man it shall not live when the trumpet soundeth long they shall come up to the mount 14 And Moses went down from the mount unto the people and sanctified the people and they washed their clothes 15 And he said unto the people Be ready against the third day come wives 16 And it came to pass on the third day in the morning that there were thunders and lightnings and a thick cloud upon the mount and the voice of the trumpet exceeding loud so that all the people in the camp trembled 17 And Moses brought forth the people out of the camp to meet with God and they stood at the nether part of the mount 18 And mount Sinai was altogether on a smoke because descended upon it in fire and the smoke thereof ascended as the smoke of a furnace and the whole mount quaked greatly 19 And when the voice of the trumpet sounded long and waxed louder and louder Moses spake and God answered him by a voice 20 came down upon mount Sinai on the top of the mount called Moses to the top of the mount and Moses went up 21 said unto Moses Go down charge the people lest they break through to gaze and many of them perish 22 And let the priests also, which come near sanctify break forth 23 And Moses said The people cannot come up to mount Sinai for thou chargedst us, saying Set bounds about the mount and sanctify 24 said unto him, Away get thee down and thou shalt come up thou, and Aaron with thee: but let not the priests and the people break through to come up lest he break forth 25 So Moses went down unto the people and spake
