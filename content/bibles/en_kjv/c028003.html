3

1 Then said unto me, Go yet, love a woman beloved friend yet an adulteress according to the love toward the children of Israel who look to other gods and love flagons of wine 2 So I bought her to me for fifteen of silver an homer of barley and an half homer of barley 3 And I said unto her, Thou shalt abide for me many days thou shalt not play the harlot and thou shalt not be man 4 For the children of Israel shall abide many days without a king and without a prince and without a sacrifice and without an image and without an ephod teraphim 5 Afterward shall the children of Israel return and seek their God and David their king and shall fear and his goodness in the latter days
