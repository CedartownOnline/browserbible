2

1 Woe to them that devise iniquity and work evil upon their beds when the morning is light they practise it, because it is in the power of their hand 2 And they covet fields by violence and houses away so they oppress a man and his house even a man and his heritage 3 Therefore thus saith Behold, against this family do I devise an evil from which ye shall not remove your necks neither shall ye go haughtily for this time evil 4 In that day take up a parable against you, and lament with a doleful lamentation say We be utterly spoiled he hath changed the portion of my people how hath he removed from me! turning away he hath divided our fields 5 Therefore thou shalt have none that shall cast a cord by lot in the congregation 6 Prophesy prophesy they shall not prophesy they shall not take shame 7 named the house of Jacob is the spirit straitened these his doings do not my words do good to him that walketh uprightly 8 Even of late my people is risen up as an enemy ye pull off the robe with the garment from them that pass by securely as men averse from war 9 The women of my people have ye cast out from their pleasant houses from their children have ye taken away my glory for ever 10 Arise ye, and depart rest because it is polluted it shall destroy even with a sore destruction 11 If a man walking in the spirit and falsehood do lie I will prophesy unto thee of wine and of strong drink he shall even be the prophet of this people 12 I will surely assemble O Jacob all of thee; I will surely gather the remnant of Israel I will put them together as the sheep of Bozrah as the flock in the midst of their fold they shall make great noise men 13 The breaker is come up before them: they have broken up and have passed through the gate and are gone out by it: and their king shall pass before on the head
