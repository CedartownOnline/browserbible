23

1 And it came to pass a long time after had given rest unto Israel from all their enemies round about that Joshua waxed old stricken in age 2 And Joshua called for all Israel for their elders and for their heads and for their judges and for their officers and said unto them, I am old stricken in age 3 And ye have seen your God hath done unto all these nations because your God he that hath fought 4 Behold I have divided unto you by lot these nations that remain to be an inheritance for your tribes from Jordan with all the nations that I have cut off even unto the great sea westward 5 your God he shall expel them from before you, and drive them from out of your sight and ye shall possess their land your God hath promised 6 Be ye therefore very courageous to keep and to do all that is written in the book of the law of Moses that ye turn the right hand the left 7 That ye come not among these nations these that remain among you; neither make mention of the name of their gods nor cause to swear neither serve them, nor bow 8 But cleave your God as ye have done unto this day 9 hath driven out from before you great nations and strong you, no man hath been able to stand before you unto this day 10 One man of you shall chase a thousand your God that fighteth for you, as he hath promised 11 Take good heed therefore unto yourselves that ye love your God 12 Else if ye do in any wise go back and cleave unto the remnant of these nations these that remain among you, and shall make marriages with them, and go in 13 Know for a certainty your God will no more drive out these nations from before you; but they shall be snares and traps unto you, and scourges in your sides and thorns in your eyes until ye perish from off this good land your God hath given 14 And, behold, this day going the way of all the earth and ye know in all your hearts and in all your souls that not one thing hath failed of all the good things your God spake concerning you; all are come to pass not one thing hath failed 15 as all good things are come your God promised bring upon you all evil things until he have destroyed you from off this good land your God hath given 16 When ye have transgressed the covenant your God which he commanded you, and have gone and served other gods and bowed yourselves to them; then shall the anger be kindled against you, and ye shall perish quickly from off the good land which he hath given
