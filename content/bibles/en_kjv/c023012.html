12

1 And in that day thou shalt say I will praise thee: though thou wast angry with me, thine anger is turned away and thou comfortedst 2 Behold, God my salvation I will trust and not be afraid for the LORD my strength song he also is become my salvation 3 Therefore with joy shall ye draw water out of the wells of salvation 4 And in that day shall ye say Praise call upon his name declare his doings among the people make mention that his name is exalted 5 Sing for he hath done excellent things known in all the earth 6 Cry out and shout thou inhabitant of Zion for great the Holy One of Israel in the midst
