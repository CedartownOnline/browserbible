18

1 The priests the Levites all the tribe of Levi shall have no part nor inheritance with Israel they shall eat the offerings made by fire and his inheritance 2 Therefore shall they have no inheritance among their brethren their inheritance as he hath said 3 And this shall be the priest' due from the people from them that offer a sacrifice ox or sheep and they shall give unto the priest the shoulder and the two cheeks and the maw 4 The firstfruit of thy corn of thy wine and of thine oil and the first of the fleece of thy sheep shalt thou give 5 thy God hath chosen him out of all thy tribes to stand to minister in the name him and his sons for ever 6 And if a Levite come from any of thy gates out of all Israel where he sojourned and come with all the desire of his mind unto the place shall choose 7 Then he shall minister in the name his God as all his brethren the Levites which stand there before 8 They shall have like portions to eat beside that which cometh of the sale of his patrimony 9 When thou art come into the land thy God giveth thee, thou shalt not learn to do after the abominations of those nations 10 There shall not be found that maketh his son or his daughter to pass through the fire that useth divination an observer of times or an enchanter or a witch 11 Or a charmer or a consulter with familiar spirits or a wizard or a necromancer 12 For all that do an abomination and because of these abominations thy God doth drive them out from before 13 Thou shalt be perfect thy God 14 For these nations which thou shalt possess hearkened unto observers of times and unto diviners thy God hath not suffered 15 thy God will raise up unto thee a Prophet from the midst of thee, of thy brethren like unto me; unto him ye shall hearken 16 According to all that thou desiredst thy God in Horeb in the day of the assembly saying Let me not hear again the voice my God neither let me see this great fire any more, that I die 17 said unto me, They have well which they have spoken 18 I will raise them up a Prophet from among their brethren like unto thee, and will put my words in his mouth and he shall speak unto them all that I shall command 19 whosoever will not hearken unto my words which he shall speak in my name I will require 20 But the prophet which shall presume to speak a word in my name which I have not commanded him to speak or that shall speak in the name of other gods even that prophet shall die 21 And if thou say in thine heart How shall we know the word hath not spoken 22 When a prophet speaketh in the name if the thing follow not, nor come to pass the thing hath not spoken the prophet hath spoken it presumptuously thou shalt not be afraid
