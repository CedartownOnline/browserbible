Deut 5

4

1 Now therefore hearken O Israel unto the statutes and unto the judgments which I teach you, for to do that ye may live and go in and possess the land God of your fathers giveth 2 Ye shall not add unto the word which I command you, neither shall ye diminish from it, that ye may keep the commandments your God which I command 3 Your eyes have seen did because of Baalpeor for all the men that followed Baalpeor thy God hath destroyed them from among 4 But ye that did cleave your God alive every one of you this day 5 Behold I have taught you statutes and judgments even as my God commanded me, that ye should do so in the land whither ye go to possess 6 Keep therefore and do your wisdom and your understanding in the sight of the nations which shall hear all these statutes and say Surely this great nation a wise and understanding people 7 For what nation great God nigh our God we call 8 And what nation great that hath statutes and judgments righteous as all this law which I set before you this day 9 Only take heed to thyself, and keep thy soul diligently lest thou forget the things which thine eyes have seen and lest they depart from thy heart all the days of thy life but teach them thy sons and thy sons sons 10 the day that thou stoodest before thy God in Horeb said unto me, Gather me the people together and I will make them hear my words that they may learn to fear me all the days that they shall live upon the earth they may teach their children 11 And ye came near and stood under the mountain and the mountain burned with fire unto the midst of heaven with darkness clouds and thick darkness 12 spake unto you out of the midst of the fire ye heard the voice of the words but saw no similitude only a voice 13 And he declared unto you his covenant which he commanded you to perform ten commandments and he wrote them upon two tables of stone 14 commanded me at that time to teach you statutes and judgments that ye might do them in the land whither ye go over to possess 15 Take ye therefore good heed unto yourselves for ye saw no manner of similitude on the day spake unto you in Horeb out of the midst of the fire 16 Lest ye corrupt and make you a graven image the similitude of any figure the likeness of male or female 17 The likeness of any beast on the earth the likeness of any winged fowl that flieth in the air 18 The likeness of any thing that creepeth on the ground the likeness of any fish in the waters beneath the earth 19 And lest thou lift up thine eyes unto heaven and when thou seest the sun and the moon and the stars all the host of heaven shouldest be driven to worship them, and serve thy God hath divided unto all nations under the whole heaven 20 hath taken you, and brought you forth out of the iron furnace out of Egypt to be unto him a people of inheritance this day 21 was angry with me for your sakes and sware that I should not go over Jordan and that I should not go in unto that good land thy God giveth an inheritance 22 But I must die in this land I must not go over Jordan but ye shall go over and possess that good land 23 Take heed unto yourselves, lest ye forget the covenant your God which he made with you, and make you a graven image the likeness of any thy God hath forbidden 24 thy God a consuming fire a jealous God 25 When thou shalt beget children and children' children and ye shall have remained long in the land and shall corrupt and make a graven image the likeness and shall do evil in the sight thy God to provoke him to anger 26 I call heaven and earth to witness against you this day that ye shall soon utterly perish from off the land whereunto ye go over Jordan to possess it; ye shall not prolong days upon it, but shall utterly be destroyed 27 shall scatter you among the nations and ye shall be left few in number among the heathen shall lead 28 And there ye shall serve gods the work of men' hands wood and stone which neither see nor hear nor eat nor smell 29 But if from thence thou shalt seek thy God thou shalt find if thou seek him with all thy heart and with all thy soul 30 When thou art in tribulation and all these things are come in the latter days if thou turn thy God and shalt be obedient unto his voice 31 thy God a merciful God he will not forsake thee, neither destroy thee, nor forget the covenant of thy fathers which he sware 32 For ask now of the days that are past which were before thee, since the day that God created man upon the earth from the one side of heaven as this great thing or hath been heard 33 people hear the voice of God speaking out of the midst of the fire as thou hast heard and live 34 Or hath God assayed to go take him a nation from the midst nation by temptations by signs and by wonders and by war and by a mighty hand and by a stretched out arm and by great terrors your God did for you in Egypt before your eyes 35 Unto thee it was shewed that thou mightest know God none else beside him 36 Out of heaven he made thee to hear his voice that he might instruct thee: and upon earth he shewed thee his great fire and thou heardest his words out of the midst of the fire 37 And because he loved thy fathers therefore he chose their seed after them, and brought thee out in his sight with his mighty power out of Egypt 38 To drive out nations from before thee greater and mightier to bring thee in, to give thee their land an inheritance this day 39 Know therefore this day and consider in thine heart God in heaven above and upon the earth 40 Thou shalt keep therefore his statutes and his commandments which I command thee this day that it may go well with thee, and with thy children after thee, and that thou mayest prolong days upon the earth thy God giveth 41 Then Moses severed three cities on this side Jordan toward the sunrising 42 That the slayer might flee thither, which should kill his neighbour unawares and hated him not in times past and that fleeing unto one of these cities he might live 43 Bezer in the wilderness in the plain country of the Reubenites and Ramoth in Gilead of the Gadites and Golan in Bashan of the Manassites 44 the law which Moses set before the children of Israel 45 the testimonies and the statutes and the judgments which Moses spake unto the children of Israel after they came forth out of Egypt 46 On this side Jordan in the valley over against Bethpeor in the land of Sihon king of the Amorites who dwelt at Heshbon whom Moses and the children of Israel smote after they were come forth out of Egypt 47 And they possessed his land and the land of Og king of Bashan two kings of the Amorites on this side Jordan toward the sunrising 48 From Aroer by the bank of the river Arnon even unto mount Sion Hermon 49 And all the plain on this side Jordan eastward even unto the sea of the plain under the springs of Pisgah
