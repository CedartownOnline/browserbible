1 Elihu spake moreover, and said 2 Thinkest thou this to be right thou saidst My righteousness more than God' 3 For thou saidst What advantage What profit from my sin 4 I will answer thee, and thy companions 5 Look unto the heavens and see and behold the clouds are higher 6 If thou sinnest what doest thy transgressions be multiplied what doest 7 If thou be righteous what givest thou him? or what receiveth he of thine hand 8 Thy wickedness a man and thy righteousness the son of man 9 By reason of the multitude of oppressions to cry they cry out by reason of the arm of the mighty 10 But none saith God my maker who giveth songs in the night 11 Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth and maketh us wiser than the fowls of heaven 12 There they cry but none giveth answer because of the pride of evil men 13 Surely God will not hear vanity neither will the Almighty regard 14 Although thou sayest thou shalt not see judgment before him; therefore trust 15 he hath visited in his anger yet he knoweth not in great extremity 16 Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vain he multiplieth words without knowledge
