7

1 the law of the trespass offering most holy 2 In the place where they kill the burnt offering shall they kill the trespass offering and the blood thereof shall he sprinkle round about upon the altar 3 And he shall offer of it all the fat thereof; the rump and the fat that covereth the inwards 4 And the two kidneys and the fat by the flanks and the caul above the liver with the kidneys it shall he take away 5 And the priest shall burn them upon the altar an offering made by fire a trespass offering 6 Every male among the priests shall eat thereof: it shall be eaten in the holy place most holy 7 As the sin offering the trespass offering one law for them: the priest that maketh atonement 8 And the priest that offereth any man' burnt offering the priest shall have to himself the skin of the burnt offering which he hath offered 9 And all the meat offering that is baken in the oven and all that is dressed in the fryingpan and in the pan shall be the priest' that offereth 10 And every meat offering mingled with oil and dry shall all the sons of Aaron have, one as another 11 the law of the sacrifice of peace offerings which he shall offer 12 If he offer it for a thanksgiving then he shall offer with the sacrifice of thanksgiving unleavened cakes mingled with oil and unleavened wafers anointed with oil and cakes mingled with oil of fine flour fried 13 Besides the cakes he shall offer his offering leavened bread with the sacrifice of thanksgiving of his peace offerings 14 And of it he shall offer one out of the whole oblation an heave offering it shall be the priest' that sprinkleth the blood of the peace offerings 15 And the flesh of the sacrifice of his peace offerings for thanksgiving shall be eaten the same day that it is offered he shall not leave any of it until the morning 16 But if the sacrifice of his offering a vow or a voluntary offering it shall be eaten the same day that he offereth his sacrifice and on the morrow also the remainder of it shall be eaten 17 But the remainder of the flesh of the sacrifice on the third day shall be burnt with fire 18 of the flesh of the sacrifice of his peace offerings be eaten at all on the third day it shall not be accepted neither shall it be imputed unto him that offereth it: it shall be an abomination and the soul that eateth of it shall bear his iniquity 19 And the flesh that toucheth any unclean shall not be eaten it shall be burnt with fire and as for the flesh all that be clean shall eat 20 But the soul that eateth the flesh of the sacrifice of peace offerings having his uncleanness upon him, even that soul shall be cut off from his people 21 Moreover the soul that shall touch any unclean the uncleanness of man unclean beast or any abominable unclean and eat of the flesh of the sacrifice of peace offerings even that soul shall be cut off from his people 22 spake unto Moses saying 23 Speak unto the children of Israel saying Ye shall eat no manner of fat of ox or of sheep or of goat 24 And the fat of the beast that dieth of itself and the fat of that which is torn with beasts may be used in any other use but ye shall in no wise eat 25 For whosoever eateth the fat of the beast of which men offer an offering made by fire even the soul that eateth shall be cut off from his people 26 Moreover ye shall eat no manner of blood of fowl or of beast in any of your dwellings 27 Whatsoever soul that eateth any manner of blood even that soul shall be cut off from his people 28 spake unto Moses saying 29 Speak unto the children of Israel saying He that offereth the sacrifice of his peace offerings shall bring his oblation of the sacrifice of his peace offerings 30 His own hands shall bring the offerings made by fire the fat with the breast it shall he bring that the breast may be waved a wave offering before 31 And the priest shall burn the fat upon the altar but the breast shall be Aaron' and his sons 32 And the right shoulder shall ye give unto the priest an heave offering of the sacrifices of your peace offerings 33 He among the sons of Aaron that offereth the blood of the peace offerings and the fat shall have the right shoulder part 34 For the wave breast and the heave shoulder have I taken of the children of Israel from off the sacrifices of their peace offerings and have given them unto Aaron the priest and unto his sons by a statute for ever from among the children of Israel 35 of the anointing of Aaron and of the anointing of his sons out of the offerings made by fire in the day he presented in the priest' office 36 commanded to be given them of the children of Israel in the day that he anointed a statute for ever throughout their generations 37 the law of the burnt offering of the meat offering and of the sin offering and of the trespass offering and of the consecrations and of the sacrifice of the peace offerings 38 commanded Moses in mount Sinai in the day that he commanded the children of Israel to offer their oblations in the wilderness of Sinai
