8

1 Again the word of hosts saying 2 Thus saith of hosts I was jealous for Zion with great jealousy and I was jealous for her with great fury 3 Thus saith I am returned unto Zion and will dwell in the midst of Jerusalem and Jerusalem shall be called a city of truth and the mountain of hosts the holy mountain 4 Thus saith of hosts There shall yet old men and old women dwell in the streets of Jerusalem and every man with his staff in his hand for very age 5 And the streets of the city shall be full of boys and girls playing in the streets 6 Thus saith of hosts If it be marvellous in the eyes of the remnant of this people in these days should it also be marvellous in mine eyes saith of hosts 7 Thus saith of hosts Behold, I will save my people from the east country and from the west country 8 And I will bring them, and they shall dwell in the midst of Jerusalem and they shall be my people and I will be their God in truth and in righteousness 9 Thus saith of hosts Let your hands be strong ye that hear in these days these words by the mouth of the prophets in the day the foundation of the house of hosts was laid that the temple might be built 10 For before these days there was no hire for man nor any hire for beast peace to him that went out or came in because of the affliction for I set all men every one against his neighbour 11 unto the residue of this people as in the former days saith of hosts 12 For the seed prosperous the vine shall give her fruit and the ground shall give her increase and the heavens shall give their dew and I will cause the remnant of this people to possess 13 as ye were a curse among the heathen O house of Judah and house of Israel so will I save you, and ye shall be a blessing fear let your hands be strong 14 For thus saith of hosts As I thought to punish you, when your fathers provoked me to wrath saith of hosts and I repented 15 So again have I thought in these days to do well unto Jerusalem and to the house of Judah fear 16 the things that ye shall do Speak ye every man the truth to his neighbour execute the judgment of truth and peace in your gates 17 And let none of you imagine evil in your hearts against his neighbour and love no false oath that I hate saith 18 And the word of hosts came unto me, saying 19 Thus saith of hosts The fast of the fourth and the fast of the fifth and the fast of the seventh and the fast of the tenth shall be to the house of Judah joy and gladness and cheerful feasts therefore love the truth and peace 20 Thus saith of hosts that there shall come people and the inhabitants of many cities 21 And the inhabitants of one shall go to another saying Let us go speedily to pray before and to seek of hosts I will go 22 Yea, many people and strong nations shall come to seek of hosts in Jerusalem and to pray before 23 Thus saith of hosts In those days that ten men shall take hold out of all languages of the nations even shall take hold of the skirt of him that is a Jew saying We will go with you: for we have heard God
