7

1 Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me; and, behold, he formed grasshoppers in the beginning of the shooting up of the latter growth the latter growth after the king' mowings 2 when they had made an end of eating the grass of the land then I said O Lord GOD forgive I beseech thee: by whom shall Jacob arise small 3 repented for this: It shall not be, saith 4 Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me: and, behold, the Lord GOD called to contend by fire and it devoured the great deep and did eat up a part 5 Then said I, O Lord GOD cease I beseech thee: by whom shall Jacob arise small 6 repented for this: This also shall not be, saith the Lord GOD 7 Thus he shewed me: and, behold, the Lord stood upon a wall by a plumbline with a plumbline in his hand 8 said unto me, Amos what seest thou? And I said A plumbline Then said the Lord Behold, I will set a plumbline in the midst of my people Israel I will not again pass by 9 And the high places of Isaac shall be desolate and the sanctuaries of Israel shall be laid waste and I will rise against the house of Jeroboam with the sword 10 Then Amaziah the priest of Bethel sent to Jeroboam king of Israel saying Amos hath conspired against thee in the midst of the house of Israel the land is not able to bear all his words 11 For thus Amos saith Jeroboam shall die by the sword and Israel shall surely be led away captive out of their own land 12 Also Amaziah said unto Amos O thou seer go flee thee away into the land of Judah and there eat bread and prophesy 13 But prophesy not again any more at Bethel the king' chapel the king' court 14 Then answered Amos and said to Amaziah no prophet I a prophet' son an herdman and a gatherer of sycomore fruit 15 took me as I followed the flock said unto me, Go prophesy unto my people Israel 16 Now therefore hear thou the word Thou sayest Prophesy not against Israel and drop against the house of Isaac 17 Therefore thus saith Thy wife shall be an harlot in the city and thy sons and thy daughters shall fall by the sword and thy land shall be divided by line and thou shalt die in a polluted land and Israel shall surely go into captivity forth of his land
