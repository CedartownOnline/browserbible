1 Praise ye the LORD Praise O ye servants praise the name 2 Blessed be the name from this time forth and for evermore 3 From the rising of the sun unto the going down name to be praised 4 high above all nations his glory above the heavens 5 our God who dwelleth on high 6 Who humbleth to behold in heaven and in the earth 7 He raiseth up the poor out of the dust lifteth the needy out of the dunghill 8 That he may set with princes with the princes of his people 9 He maketh the barren woman to keep house a joyful mother of children Praise ye the LORD
