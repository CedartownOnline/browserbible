9

1 spake unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai in the first month of the second year after they were come out of the land of Egypt saying 2 Let the children of Israel also keep the passover at his appointed season 3 In the fourteenth day of this month at even ye shall keep it in his appointed season according to all the rites of it, and according to all the ceremonies thereof, shall ye keep 4 And Moses spake unto the children of Israel that they should keep the passover 5 And they kept the passover on the fourteenth day of the first month at even in the wilderness of Sinai commanded Moses so did the children of Israel 6 And there were certain men who were defiled by the dead body of a man that they could not keep the passover on that day and they came before Moses and before Aaron on that day 7 And those men said defiled by the dead body of a man wherefore are we kept back that we may not offer an offering in his appointed season among the children of Israel 8 And Moses said unto them, Stand still and I will hear will command 9 spake unto Moses saying 10 Speak unto the children of Israel saying If any man of you or of your posterity shall be unclean by reason of a dead body in a journey afar off yet he shall keep the passover 11 The fourteenth day of the second month at even they shall keep eat it with unleavened bread and bitter 12 They shall leave none of it unto the morning nor break any bone of it: according to all the ordinances of the passover they shall keep 13 But the man clean and is not in a journey and forbeareth to keep the passover even the same soul shall be cut off from among his people because he brought not the offering in his appointed season that man shall bear his sin 14 And if a stranger shall sojourn among you, and will keep the passover according to the ordinance of the passover and according to the manner thereof, so shall he do ye shall have one ordinance both for the stranger and for him that was born in the land 15 And on the day that the tabernacle was reared up the cloud covered the tabernacle the tent of the testimony and at even there was upon the tabernacle as it were the appearance of fire until the morning 16 So it was alway the cloud covered and the appearance of fire by night 17 And when the cloud was taken up from the tabernacle then after that the children of Israel journeyed and in the place where the cloud abode there the children of Israel pitched their tents 18 At the commandment the children of Israel journeyed and at the commandment they pitched as long as the cloud abode upon the tabernacle they rested 19 And when the cloud tarried long upon the tabernacle many days then the children of Israel kept the charge and journeyed 20 it was when the cloud was a few days upon the tabernacle according to the commandment they abode in their tents, and according to the commandment they journeyed 21 it was when the cloud abode from even unto the morning the cloud was taken up in the morning then they journeyed by day or by night that the cloud was taken up they journeyed 22 two days or a month or a year that the cloud tarried upon the tabernacle remaining thereon, the children of Israel abode in their tents, and journeyed not: but when it was taken up they journeyed 23 At the commandment they rested in the tents, and at the commandment they journeyed they kept the charge at the commandment by the hand of Moses
