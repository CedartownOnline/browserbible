12

1 In the seventh year of Jehu Jehoash began to reign and forty years reigned he in Jerusalem And his mother' name Zibiah of Beersheba 2 And Jehoash did right in the sight all his days wherein Jehoiada the priest instructed 3 But the high places were not taken away the people still sacrificed and burnt incense in the high places 4 And Jehoash said to the priests All the money of the dedicated things that is brought into the house the money of every one that passeth the money that every man is set at all the money that cometh into any man' heart to bring into the house 5 Let the priests take to them, every man of his acquaintance and let them repair the breaches of the house wheresoever any breach shall be found 6 in the three and twentieth year of king Jehoash the priests had not repaired the breaches of the house 7 Then king Jehoash called for Jehoiada the priest priests and said unto them, Why repair ye not the breaches of the house now therefore receive money of your acquaintance but deliver it for the breaches of the house 8 And the priests consented to receive no money of the people neither to repair the breaches of the house 9 But Jehoiada the priest took a chest and bored a hole in the lid of it, and set it beside the altar on the right side as one cometh into the house and the priests that kept the door put therein all the money brought into the house 10 when they saw much money in the chest that the king' scribe and the high priest came up and they put up in bags and told the money that was found in the house 11 And they gave the money being told into the hands of them that did the work that had the oversight of the house and they laid it out to the carpenters and builders that wrought upon the house 12 And to masons and hewers of stone and to buy timber and hewed stone to repair the breaches of the house and for all that was laid out for the house to repair 13 Howbeit there were not made for the house bowls of silver snuffers basons trumpets any vessels of gold or vessels of silver of the money brought into the house 14 But they gave that to the workmen and repaired therewith the house 15 Moreover they reckoned not with the men into whose hand they delivered the money to be bestowed on workmen for they dealt faithfully 16 The trespass money and sin money was not brought into the house it was the priests 17 Then Hazael king of Syria went up and fought against Gath and took it: and Hazael set his face to go up to Jerusalem 18 And Jehoash king of Judah took all the hallowed things that Jehoshaphat and Jehoram and Ahaziah his fathers kings of Judah had dedicated and his own hallowed things and all the gold found in the treasures of the house and in the king' house and sent to Hazael king of Syria and he went away from Jerusalem 19 And the rest of the acts of Joash and all that he did they not written in the book of the chronicles of the kings of Judah 20 And his servants arose and made a conspiracy and slew Joash in the house of Millo which goeth down to Silla 21 For Jozachar the son of Shimeath and Jehozabad the son of Shomer his servants smote him, and he died and they buried him with his fathers in the city of David and Amaziah his son reigned
