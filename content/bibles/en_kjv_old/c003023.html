23

1 spake unto Moses saying 2 Speak unto the children of Israel and say the feasts which ye shall proclaim holy convocations my feasts 3 Six days shall work be done but the seventh day the sabbath of rest an holy convocation ye shall do no work the sabbath in all your dwellings 4 the feasts holy convocations which ye shall proclaim in their seasons 5 In the fourteenth of the first month at even passover 6 And on the fifteenth day of the same month the feast of unleavened bread seven days ye must eat unleavened bread 7 In the first day ye shall have an holy convocation ye shall do no servile work 8 But ye shall offer an offering made by fire seven days in the seventh day an holy convocation ye shall do no servile work 9 spake unto Moses saying 10 Speak unto the children of Israel and say unto them, When ye be come into the land which I give unto you, and shall reap the harvest thereof, then ye shall bring a sheaf of the firstfruits of your harvest unto the priest 11 And he shall wave the sheaf before to be accepted for you: on the morrow after the sabbath the priest shall wave 12 And ye shall offer that day when ye wave the sheaf an he lamb without blemish of the first year for a burnt offering 13 And the meat offering two tenth deals of fine flour mingled with oil an offering made by fire a sweet savour and the drink offering of wine the fourth of an hin 14 And ye shall eat neither bread nor parched corn nor green ears until the selfsame day that ye have brought an offering unto your God a statute for ever throughout your generations in all your dwellings 15 And ye shall count unto you from the morrow after the sabbath from the day that ye brought the sheaf of the wave offering seven sabbaths shall be complete 16 Even unto the morrow after the seventh sabbath shall ye number fifty days and ye shall offer a new meat offering 17 Ye shall bring out of your habitations two wave loaves of two tenth deals they shall be of fine flour they shall be baken with leaven the firstfruits 18 And ye shall offer with the bread seven lambs without blemish of the first year and one young bullock and two rams a burnt offering with their meat offering and their drink offerings an offering made by fire of sweet savour 19 Then ye shall sacrifice one kid of the goats for a sin offering and two lambs of the first year for a sacrifice of peace offerings 20 And the priest shall wave them with the bread of the firstfruits a wave offering before with the two lambs they shall be holy for the priest 21 And ye shall proclaim on the selfsame day it may be an holy convocation unto you: ye shall do no servile work a statute for ever in all your dwellings throughout your generations 22 And when ye reap the harvest of your land thou shalt not make clean riddance of the corners of thy field when thou reapest neither shalt thou gather any gleaning of thy harvest thou shalt leave them unto the poor and to the stranger your God 23 spake unto Moses saying 24 Speak unto the children of Israel saying In the seventh month in the first of the month shall ye have a sabbath a memorial of blowing of trumpets, an holy convocation 25 Ye shall do no servile work but ye shall offer an offering made by fire 26 spake unto Moses saying 27 Also on the tenth of this seventh month a day of atonement it shall be an holy convocation unto you; and ye shall afflict your souls and offer an offering made by fire 28 And ye shall do no work in that same day a day of atonement to make an atonement for you before your God 29 For whatsoever soul that shall not be afflicted in that same day he shall be cut off from among his people 30 And whatsoever soul that doeth any work in that same day the same soul will I destroy from among his people 31 Ye shall do no manner of work a statute for ever throughout your generations in all your dwellings 32 unto you a sabbath of rest and ye shall afflict your souls in the ninth of the month at even from even unto even shall ye celebrate your sabbath 33 spake unto Moses saying 34 Speak unto the children of Israel saying The fifteenth day of this seventh month the feast of tabernacles seven days 35 On the first day an holy convocation ye shall do no servile work 36 Seven days ye shall offer an offering made by fire on the eighth day shall be an holy convocation unto you; and ye shall offer an offering made by fire a solemn assembly ye shall do no servile work 37 the feasts which ye shall proclaim holy convocations to offer an offering made by fire a burnt offering and a meat offering a sacrifice and drink offerings every thing upon his day 38 Beside the sabbaths and beside your gifts and beside all your vows and beside all your freewill offerings which ye give 39 Also in the fifteenth day of the seventh month when ye have gathered in the fruit of the land ye shall keep a feast seven days on the first day a sabbath and on the eighth day a sabbath 40 And ye shall take you on the first day the boughs of goodly trees branches of palm trees, and the boughs of thick trees and willows of the brook and ye shall rejoice before your God seven days 41 And ye shall keep it a feast seven days in the year a statute for ever in your generations ye shall celebrate it in the seventh month 42 Ye shall dwell in booths seven days all that are Israelites born shall dwell in booths 43 That your generations may know that I made the children of Israel to dwell in booths when I brought them out of the land of Egypt your God 44 And Moses declared unto the children of Israel the feasts
