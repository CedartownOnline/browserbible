1 O come let us sing let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation 2 Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving and make a joyful noise unto him with psalms 3 a great God and a great King above all gods 4 In his hand the deep places of the earth the strength of the hills 5 The sea his, and he made it: and his hands formed the dry 6 O come let us worship and bow down let us kneel before our maker 7 our God the people of his pasture and the sheep of his hand To day if ye will hear his voice 8 Harden not your heart as in the provocation the day of temptation in the wilderness 9 When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my work 10 Forty years long was I grieved generation and said a people that do err in their heart and they have not known my ways 11 Unto whom I sware in my wrath that they should not enter into my rest
