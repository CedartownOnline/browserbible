62

1 For Zion' sake will I not hold my peace and for Jerusalem' sake I will not rest until the righteousness thereof go forth as brightness and the salvation thereof as a lamp burneth 2 And the Gentiles shall see thy righteousness and all kings thy glory and thou shalt be called by a new name which the mouth shall name 3 Thou shalt also be a crown of glory in the hand and a royal diadem in the hand of thy God 4 Thou shalt no more be termed Forsaken neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate but thou shalt be called Hephzibah and thy land Beulah delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married 5 a young man marrieth a virgin shall thy sons marry the bridegroom rejoiceth over the bride shall thy God rejoice 6 I have set watchmen upon thy walls O Jerusalem shall never hold their peace day nor night ye that make mention keep not silence 7 And give him no rest till he establish and till he make Jerusalem a praise in the earth 8 hath sworn by his right hand and by the arm of his strength Surely I will no more give thy corn meat for thine enemies and the sons of the stranger shall not drink thy wine for the which thou hast laboured 9 But they that have gathered it shall eat it, and praise and they that have brought it together shall drink it in the courts of my holiness 10 Go through go through the gates prepare ye the way of the people cast up cast up the highway gather out the stones lift up a standard for the people 11 hath proclaimed unto the end of the world Say ye to the daughter of Zion Behold, thy salvation cometh behold, his reward with him, and his work before 12 And they shall call them, The holy people The redeemed and thou shalt be called Sought out A city not forsaken
