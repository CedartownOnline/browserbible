8

1 spake unto Moses saying 2 Speak unto Aaron and say unto him, When thou lightest the lamps the seven lamps shall give light over against the candlestick 3 And Aaron did so; he lighted the lamps thereof over against the candlestick commanded Moses 4 And this work of the candlestick beaten gold unto the shaft thereof, unto the flowers beaten work according unto the pattern had shewed Moses so he made the candlestick 5 spake unto Moses saying 6 Take the Levites from among the children of Israel and cleanse 7 And thus shalt thou do unto them, to cleanse them: Sprinkle water of purifying upon them, and let them shave all their flesh and let them wash their clothes make themselves clean 8 Then let them take a young bullock with his meat offering fine flour mingled with oil and another young bullock shalt thou take for a sin offering 9 And thou shalt bring the Levites before the tabernacle of the congregation and thou shalt gather the whole assembly of the children of Israel together 10 And thou shalt bring the Levites before and the children of Israel shall put their hands upon the Levites 11 And Aaron shall offer the Levites before an offering of the children of Israel that they may execute the service 12 And the Levites shall lay their hands upon the heads of the bullocks and thou shalt offer the one a sin offering and the other a burnt offering to make an atonement for the Levites 13 And thou shalt set the Levites before Aaron and before his sons and offer an offering 14 Thus shalt thou separate the Levites from among the children of Israel and the Levites 15 And after that shall the Levites go in to do the service of the tabernacle of the congregation and thou shalt cleanse them, and offer an offering 16 wholly given unto me from among the children of Israel instead of such as open every womb the firstborn of all the children of Israel have I taken 17 For all the firstborn of the children of Israel man and beast on the day that I smote every firstborn in the land of Egypt I sanctified 18 And I have taken the Levites for all the firstborn of the children of Israel 19 And I have given the Levites a gift to Aaron and to his sons from among the children of Israel to do the service of the children of Israel in the tabernacle of the congregation and to make an atonement for the children of Israel that there be no plague among the children of Israel when the children of Israel come nigh unto the sanctuary 20 And Moses and Aaron and all the congregation of the children of Israel did to the Levites commanded Moses concerning the Levites so did the children of Israel 21 And the Levites were purified and they washed their clothes and Aaron offered an offering before and Aaron made an atonement for them to cleanse 22 And after that went the Levites in to do their service in the tabernacle of the congregation before Aaron and before his sons had commanded Moses concerning the Levites so did 23 spake unto Moses saying 24 unto the Levites from twenty and five years old and upward they shall go in to wait upon the service of the tabernacle of the congregation 25 And from the age of fifty years they shall cease waiting upon the service and shall serve 26 But shall minister with their brethren in the tabernacle of the congregation to keep the charge and shall do no service Thus shalt thou do unto the Levites touching their charge
