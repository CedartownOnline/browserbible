1 reigneth he is clothed with majesty is clothed with strength he hath girded himself: the world also is stablished that it cannot be moved 2 Thy throne established of old from everlasting 3 The floods have lifted up the floods have lifted up their voice the floods lift up their waves 4 on high mightier than the noise of many waters the mighty waves of the sea 5 Thy testimonies are very sure holiness becometh thine house for ever
