7

1 How beautiful are your feet in sandals, prince’s daughter! 2 Your body is like a round goblet, 3 Your two breasts are like two fawns, 4 Your neck is like an ivory tower. 5 Your head on you is like Carmel. 6 How beautiful and how pleasant you are, 7 This, your stature, is like a palm tree, 8 I said, “I will climb up into the palm tree. 9 Your mouth like the best wine, 10 I am my beloved’s. 11 Come, my beloved, let us go out into the field. 12 Let’s go early up to the vineyards. 13 The mandrakes produce fragrance.
