16

1 Preserve me, God, for in you do I take refuge. 2 My soul, you have said to Yahweh, “You are my Lord. 3 As for the saints who are in the earth, 4 Their sorrows shall be multiplied who give gifts to another god. 5 Yahweh assigned my portion and my cup. 6 The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places. 7 I will bless Yahweh, who has given me counsel. 8 I have set Yahweh always before me. 9 Therefore my heart is glad, and my tongue rejoices. 10 For you will not leave my soul in Sheol+Sheol is the place of the dead., 11 You will show me the path of life.
