132

1 Yahweh, remember David and all his affliction, 2 how he swore to Yahweh, 3 “Surely I will not come into the structure of my house, 4 I will not give sleep to my eyes, 5 until I find out a place for Yahweh, 6 Behold, we heard of it in Ephrathah. 7 “We will go into his dwelling place. 8 Arise, Yahweh, into your resting place; 9 Let your priest be clothed with righteousness. 10 For your servant David’s sake, 11 Yahweh has sworn to David in truth. 12 If your children will keep my covenant, 13 For Yahweh has chosen Zion. 14 “This is my resting place forever. 15 I will abundantly bless her provision. 16 Her priests I will also clothe with salvation. 17 There I will make the horn of David to bud. 18 I will clothe his enemies with shame,
