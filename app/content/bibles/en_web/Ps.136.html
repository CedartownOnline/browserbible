Psalm 137

136

1 Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good; 2 Give thanks to the God of gods; 3 Give thanks to the Lord of lords; 4 To him who alone does great wonders; 5 To him who by understanding made the heavens; 6 To him who spread out the earth above the waters; 7 To him who made the great lights; 8 The sun to rule by day; 9 The moon and stars to rule by night; 10 To him who struck down the Egyptian firstborn; 11 And brought out Israel from among them; 12 With a strong hand, and with an outstretched arm; 13 To him who divided the Red Sea apart; 14 And made Israel to pass through its midst; 15 But overthrew Pharaoh and his army in the Red Sea; 16 To him who led his people through the wilderness; 17 To him who struck great kings; 18 And killed mighty kings; 19 Sihon king of the Amorites; 20 Og king of Bashan; 21 And gave their land as an inheritance; 22 Even a heritage to Israel his servant; 23 Who remembered us in our low estate; 24 And has delivered us from our adversaries; 25 Who gives food to every creature; 26 Oh give thanks to the God of heaven;
