Isaiah 1

8

1 Oh that you were like my brother, 2 I would lead you, bringing you into my mother’s house, 3 His left hand would be under my head. 4 I adjure you, daughters of Jerusalem, 5 Who is this who comes up from the wilderness, 6 Set me as a seal on your heart, 7 Many waters can’t quench love, 8 We have a little sister. 9 If she is a wall, 10 I am a wall, and my breasts like towers, 11 Solomon had a vineyard at Baal Hamon. 12 My own vineyard is before me. 13 You who dwell in the gardens, with friends in attendance, 14 Come away, my beloved!
