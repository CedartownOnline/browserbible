1 But in the latter days, 2 Many nations will go and say, 3 and he will judge between many peoples, 4 But they will sit every man under his vine and under his fig tree; 5 Indeed all the nations may walk in the name of their gods; 6 “In that day,” says Yahweh, 7 and I will make that which was lame a remnant, 8 You, tower of the flock, the hill of the daughter of Zion, 9 Now why do you cry out aloud? 10 Be in pain, and labor to give birth, daughter of Zion, 11 Now many nations have assembled against you, that say, 12 But they don’t know the thoughts of Yahweh, 13 Arise and thresh, daughter of Zion;
