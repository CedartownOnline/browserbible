Psalm 44

43

1 Vindicate me, God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation. 2 For you are the God of my strength. Why have you rejected me? 3 Oh, send out your light and your truth. 4 Then I will go to the altar of God, 5 Why are you in despair, my soul?
