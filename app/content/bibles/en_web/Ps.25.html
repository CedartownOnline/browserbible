Psalm 26

25

1 To you, Yahweh, do I lift up my soul. 2 My God, I have trusted in you. 3 Yes, no one who waits for you shall be shamed. 4 Show me your ways, Yahweh. 5 Guide me in your truth, and teach me, 6 Yahweh, remember your tender mercies and your loving kindness, 7 Don’t remember the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions. 8 Good and upright is Yahweh, 9 He will guide the humble in justice. 10 All the paths of Yahweh are loving kindness and truth 11 For your name’s sake, Yahweh, 12 What man is he who fears Yahweh? 13 His soul shall dwell at ease. 14 The friendship of Yahweh is with those who fear him. 15 My eyes are ever on Yahweh, 16 Turn to me, and have mercy on me, 17 The troubles of my heart are enlarged. 18 Consider my affliction and my travail. 19 Consider my enemies, for they are many. 20 Oh keep my soul, and deliver me. 21 Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, 22 Redeem Israel, God,
