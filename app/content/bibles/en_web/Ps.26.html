Psalm 27

26

1 Judge me, Yahweh, for I have walked in my integrity. 2 Examine me, Yahweh, and prove me. 3 For your loving kindness is before my eyes. 4 I have not sat with deceitful men, 5 I hate the assembly of evildoers, 6 I will wash my hands in innocence, 7 that I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard, 8 Yahweh, I love the habitation of your house, 9 Don’t gather my soul with sinners, 10 in whose hands is wickedness, 11 But as for me, I will walk in my integrity. 12 My foot stands in an even place.
