1 Cast your bread on the waters; 2 Give a portion to seven, yes, even to eight; 3 If the clouds are full of rain, they empty themselves on the earth; 4 He who observes the wind won’t sow; 5 As you don’t know what is the way of the wind, 6 In the morning sow your seed, 7 Truly the light is sweet, 8 Yes, if a man lives many years, let him rejoice in them all; 9 Rejoice, young man, in your youth, 10 Therefore remove sorrow from your heart,
