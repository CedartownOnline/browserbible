Psalm 102

101

1 I will sing of loving kindness and justice. 2 I will be careful to live a blameless life. 3 I will set no vile thing before my eyes. 4 A perverse heart will be far from me. 5 I will silence whoever secretly slanders his neighbor. 6 My eyes will be on the faithful of the land, 7 He who practices deceit won’t dwell within my house. 8 Morning by morning, I will destroy all the wicked of the land;
