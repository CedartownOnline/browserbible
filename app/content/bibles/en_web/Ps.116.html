116

1 I love Yahweh, because he listens to my voice, 2 Because he has turned his ear to me, 3 The cords of death surrounded me, 4 Then I called on Yahweh’s name: 5 Yahweh is Gracious and righteous. 6 Yahweh preserves the simple. 7 Return to your rest, my soul, 8 For you have delivered my soul from death, 9 I will walk before Yahweh in the land of the living. 10 I believed, therefore I said, 11 I said in my haste, 12 What will I give to Yahweh for all his benefits toward me? 13 I will take the cup of salvation, and call on Yahweh’s name. 14 I will pay my vows to Yahweh, 15 Precious in the sight of Yahweh is the death of his saints. 16 Yahweh, truly I am your servant. 17 I will offer to you the sacrifice of thanksgiving, 18 I will pay my vows to Yahweh, 19 in the courts of Yahweh’s house,
