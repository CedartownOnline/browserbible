Psalm 21

20

1 May Yahweh answer you in the day of trouble. 2 send you help from the sanctuary, 3 remember all your offerings, 4 May He grant you your heart’s desire, 5 We will triumph in your salvation. 6 Now I know that Yahweh saves his anointed. 7 Some trust in chariots, and some in horses, 8 They are bowed down and fallen, 9 Save, Yahweh!
