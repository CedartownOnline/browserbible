8

1 spake unto Moses saying 2 Take Aaron and his sons with him, and the garments and the anointing oil and a bullock for the sin offering and two rams and a basket of unleavened bread 3 And gather thou all the congregation together unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation 4 And Moses did commanded him; and the assembly was gathered together unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation 5 And Moses said unto the congregation the thing commanded to be done 6 And Moses brought Aaron and his sons and washed them with water 7 And he put upon him the coat and girded him with the girdle and clothed him with the robe and put the ephod upon him, and he girded him with the curious girdle of the ephod and bound 8 And he put the breastplate upon him: also he put in the breastplate the Urim and the Thummim 9 And he put the mitre upon his head also upon the mitre upon his forefront did he put the golden plate the holy crown commanded Moses 10 And Moses took the anointing oil and anointed the tabernacle therein, and sanctified 11 And he sprinkled thereof upon the altar seven times and anointed the altar and all his vessels both the laver and his foot to sanctify 12 And he poured of the anointing oil upon Aaron' head and anointed him, to sanctify 13 And Moses brought Aaron' sons and put coats upon them, and girded them with girdles and put bonnets commanded Moses 14 And he brought the bullock for the sin offering and Aaron and his sons laid their hands upon the head of the bullock for the sin offering 15 And he slew and Moses took the blood and put upon the horns of the altar round about with his finger and purified the altar and poured the blood at the bottom of the altar and sanctified it, to make reconciliation 16 And he took all the fat upon the inwards and the caul the liver and the two kidneys and their fat and Moses burned upon the altar 17 But the bullock and his hide his flesh and his dung he burnt with fire without the camp commanded Moses 18 And he brought the ram for the burnt offering and Aaron and his sons laid their hands upon the head of the ram 19 And he killed and Moses sprinkled the blood upon the altar round about 20 And he cut the ram into pieces and Moses burnt the head and the pieces and the fat 21 And he washed the inwards and the legs in water and Moses burnt the whole ram upon the altar a burnt sacrifice for a sweet savour an offering made by fire commanded Moses 22 And he brought the other ram the ram of consecration and Aaron and his sons laid their hands upon the head of the ram 23 And he slew and Moses took of the blood of it, and put upon the tip of Aaron' right ear and upon the thumb of his right hand and upon the great toe of his right foot 24 And he brought Aaron' sons and Moses put of the blood upon the tip of their right ear and upon the thumbs of their right hands and upon the great toes of their right feet and Moses sprinkled the blood upon the altar round about 25 And he took the fat and the rump and all the fat upon the inwards and the caul the liver and the two kidneys and their fat and the right shoulder 26 And out of the basket of unleavened bread before he took one unleavened cake and a cake of oiled bread, and one wafer and put on the fat and upon the right shoulder 27 And he put all upon Aaron' hands and upon his sons hands and waved a wave offering before 28 And Moses took them from off their hands and burnt on the altar upon the burnt offering consecrations for a sweet savour an offering made by fire 29 And Moses took the breast and waved a wave offering before of the ram of consecration it was Moses part commanded Moses 30 And Moses took of the anointing oil and of the blood upon the altar and sprinkled upon Aaron upon his garments and upon his sons and upon his sons garments with him; and sanctified Aaron his garments and his sons and his sons garments 31 And Moses said unto Aaron and to his sons Boil the flesh the door of the tabernacle of the congregation and there eat it with the bread in the basket of consecrations as I commanded saying Aaron and his sons shall eat 32 And that which remaineth of the flesh and of the bread shall ye burn with fire 33 And ye shall not go out of the door of the tabernacle of the congregation seven days until the days of your consecration be at an end for seven days shall he consecrate 34 As he hath done this day hath commanded to do to make an atonement 35 Therefore shall ye abide the door of the tabernacle of the congregation day and night seven days and keep the charge that ye die not: for so I am commanded 36 So Aaron and his sons did all things commanded by the hand of Moses
