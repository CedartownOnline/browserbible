9

1 Hear O Israel to pass over Jordan this day to go in to possess nations greater and mightier than thyself, cities great and fenced up to heaven 2 A people great and tall the children of the Anakims whom thou knowest thou hast heard Who can stand before the children of Anak 3 Understand therefore this day thy God he which goeth over before a consuming fire he shall destroy them, and he shall bring them down before thy face so shalt thou drive them out and destroy them quickly hath said 4 Speak not thou in thine heart thy God hath cast them out from before thee, saying For my righteousness hath brought me in to possess this land but for the wickedness of these nations doth drive them out from before 5 Not for thy righteousness or for the uprightness of thine heart dost thou go to possess their land but for the wickedness of these nations thy God doth drive them out from before thee, and that he may perform the word sware unto thy fathers Abraham Isaac and Jacob 6 Understand thy God giveth thee not this good land to possess it for thy righteousness a stiffnecked people 7 Remember forget thy God to wrath in the wilderness from the day that thou didst depart out of the land of Egypt until ye came unto this place ye have been rebellious against 8 Also in Horeb ye provoked to wrath was angry with you to have destroyed 9 When I was gone up into the mount to receive the tables of stone the tables of the covenant made with you, then I abode in the mount forty days and forty nights I neither did eat bread nor drink water 10 delivered unto me two tables of stone written with the finger of God according to all the words spake with you in the mount out of the midst of the fire in the day of the assembly 11 And it came to pass at the end of forty days and forty nights gave me the two tables of stone the tables of the covenant 12 said unto me, Arise get thee down quickly from hence; for thy people which thou hast brought forth out of Egypt have corrupted they are quickly turned aside out of the way which I commanded them; they have made them a molten image 13 spake unto me, saying I have seen this people a stiffnecked people 14 Let me alone that I may destroy them, and blot out their name from under heaven and I will make of thee a nation mightier and greater 15 So I turned and came down from the mount and the mount burned with fire and the two tables of the covenant in my two hands 16 And I looked and, behold, ye had sinned your God had made you a molten calf ye had turned aside quickly out of the way had commanded 17 And I took the two tables and cast them out of my two hands and brake them before your eyes 18 And I fell down before as at the first forty days and forty nights I did neither eat bread nor drink water because of all your sins which ye sinned in doing wickedly in the sight to provoke him to anger 19 For I was afraid of the anger and hot displeasure was wroth against you to destroy hearkened unto me at that time 20 was very angry with Aaron to have destroyed him: and I prayed for Aaron also the same time 21 And I took your sin the calf which ye had made and burnt it with fire and stamped ground very small until it was as small as dust and I cast the dust thereof into the brook that descended out of the mount 22 And at Taberah and at Massah and at Kibrothhattaavah to wrath 23 sent you from Kadeshbarnea saying Go up and possess the land which I have given you; then ye rebelled against the commandment your God and ye believed him not, nor hearkened to his voice 24 Ye have been rebellious from the day that I knew 25 Thus I fell down before forty days and forty nights as I fell down had said he would destroy 26 I prayed and said O Lord GOD destroy not thy people and thine inheritance which thou hast redeemed through thy greatness which thou hast brought forth out of Egypt with a mighty hand 27 Remember thy servants Abraham Isaac and Jacob look not unto the stubbornness of this people nor to their wickedness nor to their sin 28 Lest the land whence thou broughtest us out say was not able to bring them into the land which he promised them, and because he hated them, he hath brought them out to slay them in the wilderness 29 thy people and thine inheritance which thou broughtest out by thy mighty power and by thy stretched out arm
