Mic 7

6

1 Hear saith Arise contend thou before the mountains and let the hills hear thy voice 2 Hear ye, O mountains controversy and ye strong foundations of the earth hath a controversy with his people and he will plead with Israel 3 O my people what have I done unto thee? and wherein have I wearied thee? testify 4 For I brought thee up out of the land of Egypt and redeemed thee out of the house of servants and I sent before thee Moses Aaron and Miriam 5 O my people remember now what Balak king of Moab consulted and what Balaam the son of Beor answered him from Shittim unto Gilgal that ye may know the righteousness 6 Wherewith shall I come before bow myself before the high God shall I come before him with burnt offerings with calves of a year old 7 be pleased with thousands of rams with ten thousands of rivers of oil shall I give my firstborn my transgression the fruit of my body the sin of my soul 8 He hath shewed thee, O man good require of thee, but to do justly and to love mercy and to walk humbly with thy God 9 voice crieth unto the city wisdom shall see thy name hear ye the rod and who hath appointed 10 Are there yet the treasures of wickedness in the house of the wicked and the scant measure abominable 11 pure with the wicked balances and with the bag of deceitful weights 12 For the rich men thereof are full of violence and the inhabitants thereof have spoken lies and their tongue deceitful in their mouth 13 sick in smiting desolate because of thy sins 14 Thou shalt eat but not be satisfied and thy casting down in the midst of thee; and thou shalt take hold but shalt not deliver which thou deliverest will I give up to the sword 15 Thou shalt sow but thou shalt not reap thou shalt tread the olives but thou shalt not anoint thee with oil and sweet wine but shalt not drink wine 16 For the statutes of Omri are kept and all the works of the house of Ahab and ye walk in their counsels that I should make thee a desolation and the inhabitants thereof an hissing therefore ye shall bear the reproach of my people
