1 And the children of Israel did evil again in the sight delivered them into the hand of the Philistines forty years 2 And there was a certain man of Zorah of the family of the Danites whose name Manoah and his wife barren and bare 3 And the angel appeared unto the woman and said barren and bearest not: but thou shalt conceive and bear a son 4 Now therefore beware I pray thee, and drink not wine nor strong drink and eat not any unclean 5 For, lo, thou shalt conceive and bear a son and no razor shall come on his head for the child shall be a Nazarite unto God from the womb and he shall begin to deliver Israel out of the hand of the Philistines 6 Then the woman came and told her husband saying A man of God came unto me, and his countenance like the countenance of an angel of God very terrible but I asked neither told he me his name 7 But he said unto me, Behold, thou shalt conceive and bear a son and now drink no wine nor strong drink neither eat any unclean for the child shall be a Nazarite to God from the womb to the day of his death 8 Then Manoah intreated and said O my Lord let the man of God which thou didst send come again unto us, and teach us what we shall do unto the child that shall be born 9 And God hearkened to the voice of Manoah and the angel of God came again unto the woman as she sat in the field but Manoah her husband 10 And the woman made haste and ran and shewed her husband and said unto him, Behold, the man hath appeared unto me, that came day 11 And Manoah arose and went after his wife and came to the man and said thou the man that spakest unto the woman And he said 12 And Manoah said Now let thy words come to pass How shall we order the child shall we do 13 And the angel said unto Manoah Of all that I said unto the woman let her beware 14 She may not eat that cometh of the vine neither let her drink wine or strong drink nor eat any unclean all that I commanded her let her observe 15 And Manoah said unto the angel I pray thee, let us detain thee, until we shall have made ready a kid for 16 And the angel said unto Manoah Though thou detain me, I will not eat of thy bread and if thou wilt offer a burnt offering thou must offer For Manoah knew an angel 17 And Manoah said unto the angel thy name that when thy sayings come to pass we may do thee honour 18 And the angel said unto him, Why askest thou thus after my name secret 19 So Manoah took a kid with a meat offering and offered upon a rock did wondrously and Manoah and his wife looked on 20 For it came to pass, when the flame went up toward heaven from off the altar that the angel ascended in the flame of the altar And Manoah and his wife looked on and fell on their faces to the ground 21 But the angel did no more appear to Manoah and to his wife Then Manoah knew an angel 22 And Manoah said unto his wife We shall surely die because we have seen God 23 But his wife said unto him, If were pleased to kill us, he would not have received a burnt offering and a meat offering at our hands neither would he have shewed nor would as at this time have told as these 24 And the woman bare a son and called his name Samson and the child grew blessed 25 And the Spirit began to move him at times in the camp of Dan between Zorah and Eshtaol
