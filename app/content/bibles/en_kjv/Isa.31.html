Isa 32

31

1 Woe to them that go down to Egypt for help and stay on horses and trust in chariots many and in horsemen because they are very strong but they look not unto the Holy One of Israel neither seek 2 wise and will bring evil and will not call back his words but will arise against the house of the evildoers and against the help of them that work iniquity 3 Now the Egyptians men and not God and their horses flesh and not spirit shall stretch out his hand both he that helpeth shall fall and he that is holpen shall fall down and they all shall fail together 4 spoken unto me, Like as the lion and the young lion roaring on his prey when a multitude of shepherds is called forth will not be afraid of their voice nor abase himself for the noise of hosts come down to fight for mount Zion and for the hill 5 As birds flying of hosts defend Jerusalem defending also he will deliver passing over he will preserve 6 Turn whom the children of Israel have deeply revolted 7 For in that day every man shall cast away his idols of silver and his idols of gold which your own hands have made a sin 8 Then shall the Assyrian fall with the sword not of a mighty man and the sword not of a mean man shall devour him: but he shall flee from the sword and his young men shall be discomfited 9 And he shall pass over to his strong hold for fear and his princes shall be afraid of the ensign saith whose fire in Zion and his furnace in Jerusalem
