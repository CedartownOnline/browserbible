1 And it came to pass at that time that Judah went down from his brethren and turned in to a certain Adullamite whose name Hirah 2 And Judah saw there a daughter of a certain Canaanite whose name Shuah and he took her and went in 3 And she conceived and bare a son and he called his name Er 4 And she conceived again, and bare a son and she called his name Onan 5 And she yet again conceived and bare a son and called his name Shelah and he was at Chezib when she bare 6 And Judah took a wife for Er his firstborn whose name Tamar 7 And Er Judah' firstborn was wicked in the sight slew 8 And Judah said unto Onan Go in unto thy brother' wife and marry her, and raise up seed to thy brother 9 And Onan knew that the seed should not be his; and it came to pass, when he went in unto his brother' wife that he spilled on the ground lest that he should give seed to his brother 10 And the thing which he did displeased wherefore he slew 11 Then said Judah to Tamar his daughter in law Remain a widow at thy father' house till Shelah my son be grown for he said Lest peradventure he die also, as his brethren And Tamar went and dwelt in her father' house 12 And in process of time the daughter of Shuah Judah' wife died and Judah was comforted and went up unto his sheepshearers to Timnath he and his friend Hirah the Adullamite 13 And it was told Tamar saying Behold thy father in law goeth up to Timnath to shear his sheep 14 And she put her widow' garments off from her, and covered her with a vail and wrapped herself and sat in an open place by the way to Timnath for she saw that Shelah was grown and she was not given unto him to wife 15 When Judah saw her he thought an harlot because she had covered her face 16 And he turned unto her by the way and said Go to I pray thee, let me come in unto thee; (for he knew his daughter in law And she said What wilt thou give me, that thou mayest come in 17 And he said I will send a kid from the flock And she said Wilt thou give a pledge till thou send 18 And he said What pledge shall I give thee? And she said Thy signet and thy bracelets and thy staff in thine hand And he gave her, and came in unto her, and she conceived 19 And she arose and went away and laid by her vail from her, and put on the garments of her widowhood 20 And Judah sent the kid by the hand of his friend the Adullamite to receive pledge from the woman' hand but he found her 21 Then he asked the men of that place saying the harlot openly by the way side And they said There was no harlot in this 22 And he returned to Judah and said I cannot find her; and also the men of the place said there was no harlot 23 And Judah said Let her take to her, lest we be shamed behold, I sent this kid and thou hast not found 24 And it came to pass about three months after, that it was told Judah saying Tamar thy daughter in law hath played the harlot with child by whoredom And Judah said Bring her forth and let her be burnt 25 brought forth she sent to her father in law saying By the man I with child and she said Discern these, the signet and bracelets and staff 26 And Judah acknowledged and said She hath been more righteous than I; because that I gave her not to Shelah my son And he knew her again 27 And it came to pass in the time of her travail that, behold, twins in her womb 28 And it came to pass, when she travailed put out hand and the midwife took and bound upon his hand a scarlet thread saying This came out first 29 And it came to pass, as he drew back his hand that, behold, his brother came out and she said How hast thou broken forth breach upon thee: therefore his name was called Pharez 30 And afterward came out his brother that had the scarlet thread upon his hand and his name was called Zarah
