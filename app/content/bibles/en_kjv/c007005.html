through the lattice Why is his chariot long in coming why tarry the wheels of his chariots 29 Her wise ladies answered her, yea, she returned answer 30 Have they not sped divided the prey to every man a damsel two to Sisera a prey of divers colours a prey of divers colours of needlework of divers colours of needlework on both sides for the necks the spoil 31 So let all thine enemies perish them
