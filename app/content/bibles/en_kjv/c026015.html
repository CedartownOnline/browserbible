Ezek 16

15

1 And the word came unto me, saying 2 Son of man What is the vine tree more than any tree a branch which is among the trees of the forest 3 Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work take a pin of it to hang any vessel 4 Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned Is it meet work 5 Behold, when it was whole it was meet for no work how much less shall it be meet work when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned 6 Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD As the vine tree among the trees of the forest which I have given to the fire for fuel so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem 7 And I will set my face against them; they shall go out fire fire shall devour them; and ye shall know when I set my face 8 And I will make the land desolate because they have committed a trespass saith the Lord GOD
