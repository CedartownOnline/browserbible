1Sam 1

4

1 Then went Boaz up to the gate and sat him down there: and, behold, the kinsman of whom Boaz spake came by; unto whom he said Ho such a one turn aside sit down here. And he turned aside and sat down 2 And he took ten men of the elders of the city and said Sit ye down here. And they sat down 3 And he said unto the kinsman Naomi that is come again out of the country of Moab selleth a parcel of land our brother Elimelech' 4 And I thought to advertise thee, saying Buy before the inhabitants and before the elders of my people If thou wilt redeem redeem but if thou wilt not redeem tell me, that I may know none to redeem beside after thee. And he said I will redeem 5 Then said Boaz What day thou buyest the field of the hand of Naomi thou must buy also of Ruth the Moabitess the wife of the dead to raise up the name of the dead upon his inheritance 6 And the kinsman said I cannot redeem for myself, lest I mar mine own inheritance redeem thou my right to thyself; for I cannot redeem 7 in former time in Israel concerning redeeming and concerning changing for to confirm all things a man plucked off his shoe and gave to his neighbour a testimony in Israel 8 Therefore the kinsman said unto Boaz Buy for thee. So he drew off his shoe 9 And Boaz said unto the elders all the people witnesses this day that I have bought Elimelech' Chilion' and Mahlon' of the hand of Naomi 10 Moreover Ruth the Moabitess the wife of Mahlon have I purchased to be my wife to raise up the name of the dead upon his inheritance that the name of the dead be not cut off from among his brethren and from the gate of his place witnesses this day 11 And all the people in the gate and the elders said witnesses make the woman that is come into thine house like Rachel and like Leah which two did build the house of Israel and do thou worthily in Ephratah and be famous in Bethlehem 12 And let thy house be like the house of Pharez whom Tamar bare unto Judah of the seed shall give thee of this young woman 13 So Boaz took Ruth and she was his wife and when he went in gave her conception and she bare a son 14 And the women said unto Naomi Blessed which hath not left thee this day without a kinsman that his name may be famous in Israel 15 And he shall be unto thee a restorer life and a nourisher of thine old age for thy daughter in law which loveth thee, which is better to thee than seven sons hath born 16 And Naomi took the child and laid it in her bosom and became nurse 17 And the women her neighbours gave it a name saying There is a son born to Naomi and they called his name Obed the father of Jesse the father of David 18 the generations of Pharez Pharez begat Hezron 19 And Hezron begat Ram and Ram begat Amminadab 20 And Amminadab begat Nahshon and Nahshon begat Salmon 21 And Salmon begat Boaz and Boaz begat Obed 22 And Obed begat Jesse and Jesse begat David
