24

1 spake unto Moses saying 2 Command the children of Israel that they bring unto thee pure oil olive beaten for the light to cause the lamps to burn continually 3 Without the vail of the testimony in the tabernacle of the congregation shall Aaron order it from the evening unto the morning before continually a statute for ever in your generations 4 He shall order the lamps upon the pure candlestick before continually 5 And thou shalt take fine flour and bake twelve cakes thereof: two tenth deals shall be in one cake 6 And thou shalt set them in two rows six on a row upon the pure table before 7 And thou shalt put pure frankincense row that it may be on the bread for a memorial an offering made by fire 8 Every sabbath he shall set it in order before continually from the children of Israel by an everlasting covenant 9 And it shall be Aaron' and his sons and they shall eat it in the holy place most holy unto him of the offerings made by fire by a perpetual statute 10 And the son of an Israelitish woman whose father an Egyptian went out among the children of Israel and this son of the Israelitish and a man of Israel strove together in the camp 11 And the Israelitish woman' son blasphemed the name and cursed And they brought him unto Moses and his mother' name Shelomith the daughter of Dibri of the tribe of Dan 12 And they put him in ward that the mind might be shewed 13 spake unto Moses saying 14 Bring forth him that hath cursed without the camp and let all that heard lay their hands upon his head and let all the congregation stone 15 And thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel saying Whosoever curseth his God shall bear his sin 16 And he that blasphemeth the name he shall surely be put to death all the congregation shall certainly stone him: as well the stranger as he that is born in the land when he blasphemeth the name shall be put to death 17 And he that killeth any man shall surely be put to death 18 And he that killeth a beast shall make it good beast for beast 19 And if a man cause a blemish in his neighbour as he hath done so shall it be done 20 Breach for breach eye for eye tooth for tooth as he hath caused a blemish in a man so shall it be done 21 And he that killeth a beast he shall restore it: and he that killeth a man he shall be put to death 22 Ye shall have one manner of law as well for the stranger as for one of your own country your God 23 And Moses spake to the children of Israel that they should bring forth him that had cursed out of the camp and stone him with stones And the children of Israel did commanded Moses
