42

1 Then he brought me forth into the utter court the way toward the north and he brought me into the chamber over against the separate place before the building toward the north 2 Before the length of an hundred cubits the north door and the breadth fifty cubits 3 Over against the twenty for the inner court and over against the pavement for the utter court gallery against gallery in three 4 And before the chambers a walk of ten cubits breadth inward a way of one cubit and their doors toward the north 5 Now the upper chambers shorter for the galleries were higher than these than the lower and than the middlemost of the building 6 in three but had not pillars as the pillars of the courts was straitened more than the lowest and the middlemost from the ground 7 And the wall without over against the chambers toward the utter court on the forepart of the chambers the length fifty cubits 8 For the length of the chambers in the utter court fifty cubits and, lo, before the temple an hundred cubits 9 And from under these chambers the entry on the east side as one goeth into them from the utter court 10 The chambers in the thickness of the wall of the court toward the east over against the separate place and over against the building 11 And the way before like the appearance of the chambers toward the north as long as as broad as they: and all their goings out both according to their fashions and according to their doors 12 And according to the doors of the chambers toward the south a door in the head of the way the way directly before the wall toward the east as one entereth 13 Then said he unto me, The north chambers the south chambers before the separate place holy chambers where the priests that approach shall eat the most holy things there shall they lay the most holy things and the meat offering and the sin offering and the trespass offering for the place holy 14 When the priests enter therein, then shall they not go out of the holy into the utter court but there they shall lay their garments wherein they minister holy and shall put on other garments and shall approach for the people 15 Now when he had made an end of measuring the inner house he brought me forth toward the gate whose prospect toward the east and measured it round about 16 He measured the east side with the measuring reed five hundred reeds with the measuring reed round about 17 He measured the north side five hundred reeds with the measuring reed round about 18 He measured the south side five hundred reeds with the measuring reed 19 He turned about to the west side measured five hundred reeds with the measuring reed 20 He measured it by the four sides it had a wall round about five hundred long and five hundred broad to make a separation between the sanctuary and the profane place
