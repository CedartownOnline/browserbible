114

1 When Israel went out of Egypt the house of Jacob from a people of strange language 2 Judah was his sanctuary Israel his dominion 3 The sea saw and fled Jordan was driven back 4 The mountains skipped like rams the little hills like lambs 5 thee, O thou sea that thou fleddest thou Jordan thou wast driven back 6 Ye mountains ye skipped like rams ye little hills like lambs 7 Tremble thou earth at the presence of the Lord at the presence of the God of Jacob 8 Which turned the rock a standing water the flint into a fountain of waters
