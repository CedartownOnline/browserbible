1 Blessed the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly nor standeth in the way of sinners nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful 2 But his delight in the law and in his law doth he meditate day and night 3 And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water that bringeth forth his fruit in his season his leaf also shall not wither and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper 4 The ungodly like the chaff which the wind driveth away 5 Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous 6 knoweth the way of the righteous but the way of the ungodly shall perish
