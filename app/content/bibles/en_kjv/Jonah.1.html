1 Now the word came unto Jonah the son of Amittai saying 2 Arise go to Nineveh that great city and cry against it; for their wickedness is come up before 3 But Jonah rose up to flee unto Tarshish from the presence and went down to Joppa and he found a ship going to Tarshish so he paid the fare thereof, and went down into it, to go with them unto Tarshish from the presence 4 sent out a great wind into the sea and there was a mighty tempest in the sea so that the ship was like to be broken 5 Then the mariners were afraid and cried every man unto his god and cast forth the wares in the ship into the sea to lighten of them. But Jonah was gone down into the sides of the ship and he lay and was fast asleep 6 So the shipmaster came to him, and said unto him, What meanest thou, O sleeper arise call upon thy God if so be that God will think upon us, that we perish 7 And they said every one to his fellow Come and let us cast lots that we may know for whose cause this evil upon us. So they cast lots and the lot fell upon Jonah 8 Then said they unto him, Tell us, we pray thee, for whose cause this evil thine occupation and whence comest thy country and of what people 9 And he said an Hebrew and I fear the God of heaven which hath made the sea and the dry 10 Then were the men exceedingly afraid and said unto him, Why hast thou done this? For the men knew that he fled from the presence because he had told 11 Then said they unto him, What shall we do unto thee, that the sea may be calm unto us? for the sea wrought and was tempestuous 12 And he said unto them, Take me up and cast me forth into the sea so shall the sea be calm unto you: for I know that for my sake this great tempest 13 Nevertheless the men rowed hard to bring to the land but they could not: for the sea wrought and was tempestuous 14 Wherefore they cried and said We beseech thee we beseech thee, let us not perish for this man' life and lay not upon us innocent blood hast done as it pleased 15 So they took up Jonah and cast him forth into the sea and the sea ceased from her raging 16 Then the men feared exceedingly and offered a sacrifice and made vows 17 had prepared a great fish to swallow up Jonah And Jonah was in the belly of the fish three days and three nights
