4

1 Hear ye children the instruction of a father and attend to know understanding 2 For I give you good doctrine forsake ye not my law 3 For I was my father' son tender and only in the sight of my mother 4 He taught me also, and said unto me, Let thine heart retain my words keep my commandments and live 5 Get wisdom get understanding forget not; neither decline from the words of my mouth 6 Forsake her not, and she shall preserve thee: love her, and she shall keep 7 Wisdom the principal thing get wisdom and with all thy getting get understanding 8 Exalt her, and she shall promote thee: she shall bring thee to honour when thou dost embrace 9 She shall give to thine head an ornament of grace a crown of glory shall she deliver 10 Hear O my son and receive my sayings and the years of thy life shall be many 11 I have taught thee in the way of wisdom I have led thee in right paths 12 When thou goest thy steps shall not be straitened and when thou runnest thou shalt not stumble 13 Take fast hold of instruction not go keep thy life 14 Enter not into the path of the wicked and go not in the way of evil 15 Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away 16 For they sleep not, except they have done mischief and their sleep is taken away to fall 17 For they eat the bread of wickedness and drink the wine of violence 18 But the path of the just as the shining light that shineth more and more unto the perfect day 19 The way of the wicked as darkness they know not at what they stumble 20 My son attend to my words incline thine ear unto my sayings 21 Let them not depart from thine eyes keep them in the midst of thine heart 22 life unto those that find them, and health to all their flesh 23 Keep thy heart with all diligence the issues of life 24 Put away from thee a froward mouth and perverse lips put far 25 Let thine eyes look right on and let thine eyelids look straight 26 Ponder the path of thy feet and let all thy ways be established 27 Turn not to the right hand nor to the left remove thy foot from evil
