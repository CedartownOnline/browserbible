27

1 spake unto Moses saying 2 Speak unto the children of Israel and say unto them, When a man shall make a singular vow the persons by thy estimation 3 And thy estimation shall be of the male from twenty years old even unto sixty years old even thy estimation shall be fifty shekels of silver after the shekel of the sanctuary 4 a female then thy estimation shall be thirty shekels 5 from five years old even unto twenty years old then thy estimation shall be of the male twenty shekels and for the female ten shekels 6 from a month old even unto five years old then thy estimation shall be of the male five shekels of silver and for the female thy estimation three shekels of silver 7 from sixty years old and above a male then thy estimation shall be fifteen shekels and for the female ten shekels 8 But if he be poorer than thy estimation then he shall present himself before the priest and the priest shall value him; according to his ability that vowed shall the priest value 9 a beast whereof men bring an offering giveth shall be holy 10 He shall not alter it, nor change it, a good for a bad or a bad for a good and if he shall at all change beast for beast then it and the exchange thereof shall be holy 11 any unclean beast of which they do not offer a sacrifice then he shall present the beast before the priest 12 And the priest shall value it, whether it be good or bad as thou valuest the priest 13 But if he will at all redeem it, then he shall add a fifth thereof unto thy estimation 14 And when a man shall sanctify his house holy then the priest shall estimate it, whether it be good or bad as the priest shall estimate it, so shall it stand 15 And if he that sanctified it will redeem his house then he shall add the fifth of the money of thy estimation 16 And if a man shall sanctify of a field of his possession then thy estimation shall be according to the seed thereof: an homer of barley seed at fifty shekels of silver 17 If he sanctify his field from the year of jubile according to thy estimation it shall stand 18 But if he sanctify his field after the jubile then the priest shall reckon unto him the money according to the years that remain even unto the year of the jubile and it shall be abated from thy estimation 19 And if he that sanctified the field will in any wise redeem it, then he shall add the fifth of the money of thy estimation unto it, and it shall be assured 20 And if he will not redeem the field or if he have sold the field to another man it shall not be redeemed 21 But the field when it goeth out in the jubile shall be holy as a field devoted the possession thereof shall be the priest' 22 sanctify a field which he hath bought not of the fields of his possession 23 Then the priest shall reckon unto him the worth of thy estimation unto the year of the jubile and he shall give thine estimation in that day a holy thing 24 In the year of the jubile the field shall return unto him of whom it was bought to him to whom the possession of the land 25 And all thy estimations shall be according to the shekel of the sanctuary twenty gerahs shall be the shekel 26 Only the firstling of the beasts firstling no man shall sanctify ox or sheep 27 of an unclean beast then he shall redeem according to thine estimation and shall add a fifth of it thereto: or if it be not redeemed then it shall be sold according to thy estimation 28 Notwithstanding no devoted thing that a man shall devote of man and beast and of the field of his possession shall be sold or redeemed every devoted thing most holy 29 None devoted which shall be devoted of men shall be redeemed shall surely be put to death 30 And all the tithe of the land of the seed of the land of the fruit of the tree holy 31 And if a man will at all redeem of his tithes he shall add thereto the fifth 32 And concerning the tithe of the herd or of the flock of whatsoever passeth under the rod the tenth shall be holy 33 He shall not search whether it be good or bad neither shall he change it: and if he change it at all then both it and the change thereof shall be holy it shall not be redeemed 34 the commandments commanded Moses for the children of Israel in mount Sinai
