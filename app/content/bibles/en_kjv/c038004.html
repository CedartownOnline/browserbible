1 And the angel that talked with me came again and waked me, as a man that is wakened out of his sleep 2 And said unto me, What seest thou? And I said I have looked and behold a candlestick gold with a bowl upon the top of it, and his seven lamps thereon, and seven pipes to the seven lamps upon the top 3 And two olive trees by it, one upon the right of the bowl and the other upon the left 4 So I answered and spake to the angel that talked with me, saying these, my lord 5 Then the angel that talked with me answered and said unto me, Knowest thou not what these be? And I said No, my lord 6 Then he answered and spake unto me, saying the word unto Zerubbabel saying Not by might nor by power but by my spirit saith of hosts 7 thou, O great mountain before Zerubbabel a plain and he shall bring forth the headstone shoutings Grace grace 8 Moreover the word came unto me, saying 9 The hands of Zerubbabel have laid the foundation of this house his hands shall also finish it; and thou shalt know of hosts hath sent 10 For who hath despised the day of small things for they shall rejoice and shall see the plummet in the hand of Zerubbabel those seven the eyes which run to and fro through the whole earth 11 Then answered I, and said these two olive trees upon the right of the candlestick and upon the left 12 And I answered again and said two olive branches which through the two golden pipes empty the golden 13 And he answered me and said Knowest And I said No, my lord 14 Then said the two anointed ones that stand by the Lord of the whole earth
