Zech 3

2

1 I lifted up mine eyes again, and looked and behold a man with a measuring line in his hand 2 Then said I, Whither goest thou? And he said unto me, To measure Jerusalem to see what the breadth the length 3 And, behold, the angel that talked with me went forth and another angel went out to meet 4 And said unto him, Run speak to this young man saying Jerusalem shall be inhabited towns without walls for the multitude of men and cattle therein 5 For I, saith will be unto her a wall of fire round about and will be the glory in the midst 6 Ho ho and flee from the land of the north saith for I have spread you abroad as the four winds of the heaven saith 7 Deliver thyself, O Zion that dwellest the daughter of Babylon 8 For thus saith of hosts After the glory hath he sent me unto the nations which spoiled you: for he that toucheth you toucheth the apple of his eye 9 For, behold, I will shake mine hand upon them, and they shall be a spoil to their servants and ye shall know of hosts hath sent 10 Sing and rejoice O daughter of Zion for, lo, I come and I will dwell in the midst of thee, saith 11 And many nations shall be joined in that day and shall be my people and I will dwell in the midst of thee, and thou shalt know of hosts hath sent 12 shall inherit Judah his portion in the holy land and shall choose Jerusalem 13 Be silent O all flesh before for he is raised up out of his holy habitation
