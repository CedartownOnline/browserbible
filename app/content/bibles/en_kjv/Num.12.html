12

1 And Miriam and Aaron spake against Moses because of the Ethiopian woman whom he had married for he had married an Ethiopian woman 2 And they said indeed spoken only by Moses hath he not spoken heard 3 Now the man Moses very meek above all the men upon the face of the earth 4 spake suddenly unto Moses and unto Aaron and unto Miriam Come out ye three unto the tabernacle of the congregation And they three came out 5 came down in the pillar of the cloud and stood the door of the tabernacle and called Aaron and Miriam and they both came forth 6 And he said Hear now my words If there be a prophet will make myself known unto him in a vision will speak unto him in a dream 7 My servant Moses faithful in all mine house 8 With him will I speak mouth to mouth even apparently and not in dark speeches and the similitude shall he behold wherefore then were ye not afraid to speak against my servant Moses 9 And the anger was kindled against them; and he departed 10 And the cloud departed from off the tabernacle and, behold, Miriam leprous as snow and Aaron looked upon Miriam leprous 11 And Aaron said unto Moses Alas my lord I beseech thee, lay not the sin upon us, wherein we have done foolishly and wherein we have sinned 12 Let her not be as one dead of whom the flesh is half consumed when he cometh out of his mother' womb 13 And Moses cried saying Heal her now, O God 14 said unto Moses If her father had but spit in her face should she not be ashamed seven days let her be shut out from the camp seven days and after that let her be received 15 And Miriam was shut out from the camp seven days and the people journeyed not till Miriam was brought 16 And afterward the people removed from Hazeroth and pitched in the wilderness of Paran
